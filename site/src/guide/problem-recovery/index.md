# Account recovery

If you've lost access to your Stello sending account, we have good and bad news.

### Good news
You can create as many sending accounts as you like and reuse the same email address. Your existing sent messages are still readable by your recipients and will expire as normal.

### Bad news
Sending accounts only store temporary sent messages, everything else stays on your device for security reasons, including the means of decrypting responses from readers. So:

 * You can't log back into your sending account
 * You can't recover any data from your sending account
 * You can't decrypt any further responses readers send
 * You can't use the same username again

If you have a backup or are able to recover your computer's harddrive then you can [restore your data](/guide/backup/) from that. Stello also backs up messages and contacts locally to `Stello Files/Backups [...]` in case you have trouble restoring Stello's data. If you were syncing your contacts with Google Contacts then you can simply sync them again.


### Account deactivation
If you lost your Stello data then you'll want to deactivate your account so that recipients don't send you replies that you can no longer access.

You'll need to [contact us](https://gracious.tech/contact) with the same email address you used for Stello. We'll need to know:

 1. Your old username
    * You can find this at the start of any link for previous messages you sent, and also in the subject of email notifications from Stello
 2. Whether you'd like to:
    * __Delete the account__: All previously sent messages disappear and recipients can no longer read them
    * __Disable the account__: Recipients can still read your old messages until they expire, but can no longer respond to them


### Manual recovery of contacts
If you don't have any backups, there's no contacts in the `Stello Files/Backups [...]` folder, and you didn't sync your contacts with Google, then there is one last way of potentially recovering your contact list.

For some email accounts, such as gmail, you will be able to recover your contact list by manually searching through the "Sent" folder in your email app. Stello sends an individual email to each recipient, so if you search for the title of your last newsletter, you should see an email for each recipient, and can recover their addresses from there.
